Our client-centred approach sets us apart. We know every person and every situation is different. Whether you are in a need of care at a short-notice, Short term accommodation or Supported independent living, we will connect with you and your family to create a service that truly works.

Our Services can be provided on an as needs basis or as an ongoing basis. We also provide respite care to allow family members who may be caring for a loved one some quality time to look after themselves or to take a well-earned break. We hope that you find the following information useful and we would be happy to arrange a free consultation to discuss how 24/7 iCare youth and community services can help you

Our Moto

Care Youth and Community services is a person-centred planning provider, and we work with all our participants to ensure they achieve the outcomes and goals important to them

iCare Youth And Community Services is registered NDIS Provider
Our services are ideal for family members looking after a loved one or for people that may need someone to care for them. We provide caring, friendly and professional Registered Nurses, Enrolled Nurses, Assistant Nurses, Carers and Companions to assist our clients to remain in the comfort of their own home and those that are in Supported independent living arrangement
Disability Services

We provide assistance for people from diverse backgrounds and with different lifestyles and personalities, with a wide range of needs.

NDIS Support Services

We understand that the NDIS can be sometimes overwhelming. That’s why we offer a range of supports, from Support Coordination to School Leaver and more.

Respite Care

Caring for someone can be very rewarding and it can also be demanding and tiring work – both physically and emotionally. It is important that family carers take time off to have a break and to look after their own health and wellbeing.

Supported Accommodation (SIL)

Find a place to live and live the life you want.For those who require 24/7 support, or significant day to day assistance.
