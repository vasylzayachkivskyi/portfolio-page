Saturday Childcare Program

2-7 years

Golden Stars Saturday Childcare Program is for children ages 2-7 years. It runs from 12-4 pm or 1-5 pm on Saturdays. During the 4-hour window, children will get free exploration, music & movement, creative arts, outdoor play, and a nutritious snack. The program is designed to give parents a break to run errands, have lunch dates, or take naps. It costs $45 per child for Golden Star families, and for the public, it costs $55 per child. You can take advantage of our special discount if you have more than one sibling enrolled in the program.

The Benefits of Sending Your Child to Preschool

Studies have shown that children who attend preschool are more likely to succeed in school than those who do not. In fact, preschool can provide your child with the skills they need to succeed in kindergarten and beyond. Learn more about some of the benefits of sending your child to preschool below.

Prepares Them for Kindergarten

Children who attend preschool are more likely to be successful in kindergarten. They are better prepared for the academic demands of school and have developed social skills that help them adjust to a new environment.

Builds Independence Skills

By being involved in a group setting, children can learn to function independently. They are encouraged to be active and take the initiative when they want something.

Helps Your Child Develops social Skills

Children learn to play and work with others. They begin to understand the concept of teamwork and cooperation, which is important in the classroom.

Enhances Your Child's Creativity

Preschool provides many opportunities for children to explore and develop creativity through play. They can experiment with new materials and ideas, which can help them in other areas of their life.

  • Sleep Therapy (Infant & Toddler)
    Golden Stars Sleep Therapy for Children is a program designed to help children sleep better. It includes a comprehensive package of strategies and tools that parents can administer at home with their children, as well as coaching sessions with an experienced sleep coach. The Golden Stars Program is grounded in the science of pediatric sleep medicine and has been shown to be effective in improving overall health, behavior, academics, moods and emotions, as well as family wellness. Our approach recognizes that all aspects of development are interconnected – physical, cognitive, social-emotional – so it’s essential to provide a holistic perspective on how kids learn best. The result? Kids who feel more rested and ready for life
  • Music Therapy (Infant & Toddler)
    Golden Stars Music Therapy for Children Program is a music-based therapy designed to help children develop their mental, emotional and physical health. It focuses on developing these skills in 3 key areas:
    • · Self-Esteem and Wellbeing
    • · Cognition and Language Development
    • · Motor Skills and Movement Control
    The program operates under the belief that by focusing on these three areas of development, we can help kids overcome developmental delays or disabilities, such as autism spectrum disorder (ASD). The Golden Stars Music Therapy Program provides individualized sessions based on what they need at that time to improve their abilities in each area of development while also helping them feel better about themselves through the therapeutic effects of music!
